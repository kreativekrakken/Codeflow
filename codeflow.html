About Me

I am a mechanical engineering graduate from NIT Kurukshetra. Whilst is college covid struck. So I knew right away that if I had to stay relevant, I had to gain some technical knowledge to survive in this digital world. Plus am more of a economics and finance geek than coding.

Thus I started first with economics courses on platforms like udemy and had some truly valuable education in that sphere. In fact, my first college level internship as a financial analyst at Goldman Sachs was also partly due to this reason and my soft skills.

But my childhood dream was to be an entrepreneur. So I thought of starting this blog to educate and also review upcoming tech. And Fintech was my most obvious choice. Thus was born ours beloved Kreative Krakken. Am glad I had support of all of you guys and hope this continues well into the future :)
